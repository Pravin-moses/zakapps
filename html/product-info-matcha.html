Our ABO Matcha is sourced exclusively from the renowned tea fields of Uji, Kyoto - the birthplace of Japanese tea culture. This ceremonial grade matcha represents the pinnacle of tea craftsmanship.

Grown in the shade for 3-4 weeks before harvest, our tea leaves develop their signature deep green color and sweet, complex flavor profile. Only the youngest, most tender leaves are hand-picked in spring.

Stone-ground to a fine powder using traditional granite mills, each 30g tin contains the essence of about 200 tea leaves. The slow grinding process (30g per hour) preserves the delicate nutrients and flavor compounds.

Certified organic by JAS (Japanese Agricultural Standards) and USDA. Free from pesticides, herbicides, and chemical fertilizers. Our tea fields have been organic for over 20 years.

PREPARATION - TRADITIONAL METHOD:

1. Sift 1-2 bamboo scoops (1.5-2g) of matcha into a bowl

2. Add 60-70ml of hot water (175¬∞F / 80¬∞C)

3. Whisk vigorously in M or W motion until frothy

4. Enjoy immediately

MODERN USES: Matcha lattes, smoothies, baking, ice cream, energy balls, face masks

INGREDIENTS: 100% ORGANIC JAPANESE MATCHA GREEN TEA POWDER

HEALTH BENEFITS: Rich in L-theanine (calm energy), EGCG antioxidants (137x more than regular green tea), chlorophyll (detoxification), vitamin C, selenium, chromium, zinc, and magnesium.

NET WEIGHT: 30g (1.06 oz) - Approximately 30 servings

STORAGE: Keep in a cool, dark place. Refrigerate after opening. Best consumed within 3 months of opening for optimal freshness and flavor.

OKAZU Chili MISO is an umami-rich chili, miso, and sesame oil based condiment often eaten with rice in Japan. This versatile sauce brings authentic Japanese flavors to any dish.

Created by a Japanese chef with decades of experience, this condiment balances heat with the deep, savory notes of organic miso paste. The combination of sunflower and sesame oils creates a silky texture that coats ingredients beautifully.

Try it as a marinade for grilled meats, mix it into your homemade ramen, or spread it on sandwiches for an umami kick. Some customers even use it as a pizza topping!

Winner of multiple culinary awards including Best Condiment at the Toronto Food Festival and featured on cooking shows across North America.

HEAT LEVEL: MILD-MEDIUM

INGREDIENTS: SUNFLOWER OIL, SESAME OIL, GARLIC, MISO PASTE (ORGANIC SOYBEANS, RICE, SALT), TAMARI SOY SAUCE (NON-GMO SOYBEANS, SALT, SUGAR), SUGAR, CHILI POWDER, WHITE SESAME SEEDS

CONTAINS: SESAME, SOYBEANS. MAY CONTAIN: MUSTARD.

PRODUCT SEPARATION IS NORMAL. REFRIGERATE AFTER OPENING. USE WITHIN 3 MONTHS FOR BEST FLAVOR.

NET WEIGHT: 230ml (7.8 fl oz)

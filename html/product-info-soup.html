Our Instant Miso Soup Tasting Set brings the authentic flavors of Japan to your kitchen with zero compromise on quality. Each packet contains premium organic miso paste, dried seaweed, tofu, and green onions.

This set includes three traditional varieties: Classic White Miso (mild and sweet), Red Miso (rich and bold), and Awase Miso (perfectly balanced blend). Each variety offers a unique taste experience.

Perfect for busy mornings, quick lunches, or as a starter for dinner. Rich in probiotics and naturally fermented for gut health. Each serving provides beneficial enzymes and essential amino acids.

Made in small batches using traditional brewing methods passed down through generations. Our miso is aged for optimal flavor development.

PREPARATION: Add contents of one packet to 1 cup (240ml) of hot water. Stir well and enjoy immediately. Do not boil after adding miso to preserve beneficial enzymes.

INGREDIENTS: ORGANIC MISO PASTE (ORGANIC SOYBEANS, RICE, SALT), DRIED WAKAME SEAWEED, FREEZE-DRIED TOFU, DRIED GREEN ONIONS, BONITO EXTRACT (DASHI)

CONTAINS: SOYBEANS, FISH. VEGAN OPTION AVAILABLE WITHOUT BONITO.

NUTRITIONAL BENEFITS: High in protein, contains probiotics, low in calories, rich in vitamins B, E, K, and folic acid.

STORE IN A COOL, DRY PLACE. SHELF STABLE FOR 18 MONTHS.
